About Me

Hi, I’m Lindsay Whipple — a data-driven storyteller with a passion for uncovering insights that inspire action.

I earned my degree in Statistics and Psychology from St. Lawrence University, where I also captained the varsity squash team and studied abroad in New Zealand. I’ve since worked at Bitsight Technologies for several years, specializing in technical research and data analysis.

Currently, I’m pursuing my Master’s in Applied Data Analytics part-time at Boston University, expanding my technical toolkit while continuing to explore the intersection of data, strategy, and communication.

Outside of work and school, I love traveling and spending time with my two dogs, Asher and Otis. Recent favorite destinations include Scottsdale, Iceland, and Lisbon — scroll through some of my favorite memories below!
